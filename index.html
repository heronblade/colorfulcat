The Artist

I'm Rachel Byler and I am the artist of The Colorful Cat Studio. I have always loved creating. I grew up around a lot of talented artists my mom and my great aunt, both encouraging me and teaching me. My love of art led me to change my major at Messiah College and I recently graduated with a BS in Art Education! I am thankful for the strong foundation Messiah gave me to grow upon and for the wide variety of media I got to learn and experience. Even though I love all types of art, painting with watercolors is my favorite. What I love about painting is that you can get lost in your work, and the flow of the brushstrokes. Its a time for me to imagine and relax. I paint a lot of nature images and portraits because I find that Gods creations are always the most beautiful. I am thoroughly enjoying Colorful Cats and I hope you can enjoy it as well!

The Studio

The Colorful Cat Studio is a collection of my own pieces as well as art that I have created for customers. The studio is easy way for anyone to bring their photos and memories to life by having them interpreted into a piece of art. Provide a photo for me to create with and then enjoy giving the work as a gift or displaying it in your own home.

The Story

In 2013 I opened The Colorful Cat Studio. It was a warm summer evening when I realized how much I had been missing painting. I pulled out all of my supplies and began sketching then painting one of my kittens. I later posted the painting online for fun and got a lot of positive feedback. It was a couple weeks later that my husband encouraged me to open an Etsy shop on the side. With that first painting in mind, so began the Coloful Cat Studio. We later decided that I should try painting full time, and so I did.
