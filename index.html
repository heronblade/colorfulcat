I'm Rachel Byler and I am the artist of The Colorful Cat Studio. I have always loved creating. I grew up around a lot of talented artists my mom and my great aunt, both encouraging me and teaching me. My love of art led me to change my major at Messiah College and I recently graduated with a BS in Art Education! I am thankful for the strong foundation Messiah gave me to grow upon and for the wide variety of Medias I got to learn and experience. Even though I love all types of art, painting with watercolors is my favorite. What I love about painting is that you can get lost in your work, and the flow of the brushstrokes. Its a time for me to imagine and relax. I paint a lot of nature images and portraits because I find that Gods creations are always the most beautiful. I am thoroughly enjoying Colorful Cats and I hope you can enjoy it as well! The Colorful Cat Studio is a collection of my own pieces as well as art that I have custom created for customer! The studio is easy way for anyone to bring their photos and memories to life by having them interpreted into a piece of art. Provide a photo for me to create with and then enjoy giving the work as a gift or displaying it in your own home.
